NOBODY

Nobody is a 2021 American action thriller film directed by Ilya Naishuller and written by Derek Kolstad. The film stars Bob Odenkirk, Connie Nielsen, Aleksei Serebryakov, RZA, Michael Ironside, Colin Salmon, and Christopher Lloyd, and follows a man who, while following clues regarding his recent home invasion, helps an underaged girl being harassed by a group of men, only to become the target of a vengeful drug lord. Odenkirk and David Leitch are among the film's producers
